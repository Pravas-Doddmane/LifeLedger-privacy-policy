Life Ledger – Account Deletion Request

How to Request Account Deletion

Life Ledger allows users to request deletion of their account and associated data. If you would like to delete your account, please follow the steps below.

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Send an email to 53cpravas@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request – Life Ledger
  3. Mention the email address used to sign in with Google

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User authentication data (Firebase account)
  • All diary entries and notes created within the Life Ledger app
  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Security questions and answers
  • All tags and categories (Special moments, Important information, Bad news)

⚠️ Warning: Once deleted, the account and associated data cannot be recovered.
