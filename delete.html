Life Ledger – Account Deletion Request

How to Request Account Deletion

Users can request deletion of their Life Ledger account and associated data by following the steps below.

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Send an email to 53cpravas@gmail.com
  2. Use subject: Account Deletion Request – Life Ledger
  3. Mention the email address used to register in the app

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User account information
  • Diary entries and notes
  • Email address and profile data
  • Stored categories and tags

⚠️ Once deleted, the account and associated data cannot be recovered.
