Privacy Policy

Personal Diary App

Last Updated: March 6, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Personal Diary App. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when registering for the app, including:

  • Email address or phone number (for account creation and password recovery)
  • Username
  • Password (encrypted and securely stored)
  • Security questions and answers (if chosen for password recovery)

2.2 Diary Content

All diary entries, notes, and content you create within the app are stored securely and privately. This includes:

  • Daily diary notes and entries
  • Dates and timestamps of entries
  • Tags and categories (Special moments, Important information, Bad news)

2.3 Authentication Data

Depending on your chosen authentication method, we may collect:

  • PIN codes (encrypted)
  • Pattern data (encrypted)
  • Biometric data (stored locally on your device only)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • Store and retrieve your diary entries
  • Provide password recovery services
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Send important notifications about your account or app updates

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • All passwords are encrypted using secure hashing algorithms
  • Diary entries are stored with end-to-end encryption
  • Biometric data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted
  • Secure servers with SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates

Note: No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

Your diary entries are retained indefinitely as per the app's core functionality - there is no option to delete diary notes. This ensures a permanent record of your life's happenings. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at the email provided below.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Email delivery (for password reset and notifications)
  • SMS services (for OTP verification)
  • Cloud storage (with encryption)
  • Analytics (anonymized data only)

These third parties have access only to the information necessary to perform their functions and are obligated not to disclose or use it for other purposes.

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request account deletion (all data will be permanently removed)
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Export your diary data

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may disclose your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
