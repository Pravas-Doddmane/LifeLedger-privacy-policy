Privacy Policy

Life Ledger – Android Application

Last Updated: March 6, 2026

Introduction

Life Ledger is an Android application developed by Mysuru Trainers and Developers. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and protected when you use the Life Ledger application.

Information We Collect

The app collects information only when users voluntarily provide it, including:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number (optional, for password recovery)
  • Username
  • Password (encrypted and securely stored)
  • Security Questions and Answers (if chosen for password recovery)
  • Daily Diary Notes and Entries
  • Dates and Timestamps of Entries
  • Tags and Categories (Special moments, Important information, Bad news)

Authentication

Life Ledger uses Google Sign-In for authentication. When signing in, Google provides basic profile information such as email address and a unique user ID, which is used only to identify your account within the app.

Additionally, the app supports alternative authentication methods including:

  • Password with security restrictions (length, symbols, uppercase letters)
  • PIN codes (encrypted)
  • Pattern locks (encrypted)
  • Biometric authentication (stored locally on your device only)

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Create, edit, and manage your diary entries
  • Search and retrieve diary notes by date, month, or category
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • Provide password recovery services via email, SMS, WhatsApp, or OTP
  • Maintain app functionality and user experience

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

Data Storage and Security

User data is stored using Google Firebase services, including Firestore and Firebase Storage. All data transfers between the app and Firebase services are protected using secure HTTPS (TLS) encryption.

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • All passwords are encrypted using secure hashing algorithms
  • Diary entries are stored with end-to-end encryption
  • Biometric data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted
  • Regular security audits and updates

While industry-standard security measures are used, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Data Deletion

Important Note: As per the core functionality of Life Ledger, there is no option to delete individual diary notes from within the app. This design ensures a permanent, safely storable record of your life's happenings. Diary entries can be modified (including empty entries) but not deleted.

However, users may request complete account and data deletion, which will permanently remove:

  • Your account information
  • All diary entries and notes
  • Authentication data
  • All associated personal information

Please note that deleted data may not be immediately removed from backend storage and may be retained for operational or maintenance purposes for a limited period.

To request account deletion, please contact us at the email address provided below or visit our Delete Account page.

Third-Party Services

Life Ledger relies on third-party services that may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies, including:

  • Google Firebase (for data storage and authentication)
  • Email/SMS/WhatsApp services (for password recovery and OTP)

For more information about Google's privacy practices, please visit:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

Life Ledger is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and are effective immediately after publication.
