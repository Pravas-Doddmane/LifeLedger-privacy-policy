Privacy Policy

Life Ledger – Android Application

Last Updated: March 6, 2026

Introduction

Life Ledger is a personal diary Android application that allows users to securely record their daily experiences. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when using the Life Ledger application.

Information We Collect

The application may collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • User account credentials
  • Diary notes and personal entries
  • Dates and timestamps of diary entries
  • Categories such as Special Moment, Important Information, or Bad News

All information is provided voluntarily by the user when using the application.

Authentication

Life Ledger uses authentication methods to protect user accounts. These may include:

  • Email and password authentication
  • Google Sign-In
  • PIN or pattern authentication
  • Biometric authentication available on supported devices

Authentication data is handled securely and is used only to identify the user within the application.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only for the following purposes:

  • Allowing users to create and manage diary entries
  • Saving and retrieving diary notes
  • Searching notes by date, month, or category
  • Authenticating user accounts
  • Providing password recovery services

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

Data Storage and Security

User data may be stored using secure cloud services such as Google Firebase.

Security measures include:

  • Secure HTTPS data transmission
  • Password encryption
  • Secure authentication methods
  • Device level biometric security where supported

While we strive to protect user data, no digital system can guarantee absolute security.

Diary Entry Policy

Life Ledger is designed as a permanent diary application. Individual diary entries cannot be deleted within the application, but they can be modified or updated by the user.

Account Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data.

To request deletion, please visit our Delete Account page.

Third-Party Services

The application may use trusted third-party services such as:

  • Google Firebase (authentication and data storage)
  • Email or SMS services for password recovery

More information about Google's privacy practices is available here:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

Life Ledger is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
